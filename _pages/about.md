I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow in the Department of Geography, The University of Hong Kong. I graduated from Department of Earth System Science, Tsinghua University (清华大学地球系统科学系) with a doctoral degree advised by Prof. Zhu Liu (刘竹) and Philippe Ciais (from LSCE), and from the School of Geography and Planning, Sun Yat-sen University (中山大学地理科学与规划学院) with a bachelor’s degree. 

I am now working on near-real-time carbon accounting, greenhouse gas budget reconciliation and sustainability data research. I have published more than 20 papers at the top international journals <a href='https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=DhtAFkwAAAAJ'><img src="https://img.shields.io/endpoint?url={{ url | url_encode }}&logo=Google%20Scholar&labelColor=f6f6f6&color=9cf&style=flat&label=citations"></a>.
